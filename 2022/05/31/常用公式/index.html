数学归纳法

$$
a_1,a_2, \cdots a_n \in \R^* : \sum \sqrt[k]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_k }<e \sum a_k
$$
加强条件
$$
\sum {k=1}^{n+1} \sqrt[k]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_k }+f(n+1)<e \sum a_k-f(n)+\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a{n+1} }+f(n+1)<e \sum_{k=1}^{n+1}a_k \\
\Leftrightarrow f(n+1)+\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n+1} }<ea_{n+1}+f(n) \\
take \quad f(n+1)=K_{n+1}\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n+1} }
$$

$$
\Leftrightarrow( K_{n+1}+1)\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n+1} }<K_n \sqrt[n]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n} }+ea_{n+1} \\
K_n=n,\\ \Leftrightarrow( n+2)\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n+1} }<n\sqrt[n]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n} }+ea_{n+1} \\
\Leftrightarrow ( n+2)\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n+1} }<(n+1)\sqrt[n+1]{a_1a_2 \cdots a_{n+1}e } \\
\Leftrightarrow n+2<(n+1) \sqrt[n+1]{e}\\
\Leftrightarrow (1+\frac{1}{n+1})^{n+1}<e
$$

调整法
$$
xyz=1, x,y,z \in R^+, \sum \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+x}} \leq \frac{3 \sqrt2}{2}
$$
fixed z，
$$
xy =\frac{1}{z}.x \leq y
$$

$$
f(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+x}} +\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+y}}\\
f’(x)=-\frac{1}{2(1+x)^{\frac{3}{2}}}-\frac{1}{2(1+y)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\cdot(-\frac{y}{x})=-\frac{1}{2x}[\frac{x}{(1+x)^{\frac{3}{2}}}- \frac{y}{(1+y)^{\frac{3}{2}}} ] \\
f’ \geq 0 \Leftrightarrow \frac{x}{(1+x)^{\frac{3}{2}}}< \frac{y}{(1+y)^{\frac{3}{2}}} \\
x^2(1+y)^3 \leq y^2(1+x)^3 \Leftrightarrow (x-y)(x+y+3xy-x^2y^2) \leq 0 \\ \Leftrightarrow x+y+3xy-x^2y^2 \geq 0 \\
\because x \leq y \leq z,xy =\frac{1}{z} \leq 1 , \Rightarrow \sum \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+x}} \leq \frac{2}{\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{\sqrt{z}}}}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+z}} \\
\Longleftrightarrow t=\frac{1}{\sqrt{z}},\quad \frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{1+t}}+1-\frac{1}{t+1} \leq \frac{3}{2}\\
\Leftrightarrow (\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+t}}-\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2})^2 \geq 0
$$
